Fully and partially spoofed audio generated with text-to-speech or voice conversion systems

The models require approximately 3 to 5 seconds of target speaker audio for voice cloning. The full-cut-seg (FCS) strategy generates fully spoofed utterances, which are then combined with bonafide segments to produce partially spoofed audio. The seg-of-word (SOW) strategy focuses on generating spoofed segments only for specific target words or phrases.

For content modification:

  • Original keywords: retain the original target words.
  • Replacing by synonym/antonym: substitute target words with their synonyms or antonyms.
  • Positive-negative restatement: rephrase sentences to reverse their affirmative or negative intent.

Among the TTS/VC models used, GlowTTS, FastPitch, Tacotron2-DDC_ph, Overflow, and Neural_HMM are not voice-cloning systems; they are single-speaker TTS models synthesising a female speaker‚Äôs voice.

We use the LibriSpeech train-clean-100 subset for the demo samples.
